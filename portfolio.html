Hi! My name is Christian Linley

Born in 1999. Raised in a mediocre town in northern Illinois. Been pursuing my passion since 2014.

I'm a Graphic Designer born and raised in Northern Illinois, now based in Bloomington, IL. I do freelance work in my spare time while pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design at Illinois State University. When not in class, I am an avid amateur photographer, with a focus mostly in portraiture and landscape photography. I am looking to intern at a design firm in the near future, where I hope to be able to use my skills to benefit the company, while gaining invaluable work experience. My main career goal is to create something that will inspire and leave a lasting impression on the mind of viewers.
