Hi! My name is Christian Linley

Born in 1999. Raised in a mediocre town in northern Illinois. Been pursuing my passion since 2014.

I'm a Graphic Designer born and raised in Northern Illinois, now based in Bloomington, IL. I do freelance work in my spare time while pursuing a BFA in Graphic Design at Illinois State University. When not in class, I am an avid amateur photographer, with a focus mostly in portraiture and landscape photography. I am looking to intern at a design firm in the near future, where I hope to be able to use my skills to benefit the company, while gaining invaluable work experience. My main career goal is to create something that will inspire and leave a lasting impression on the mind of viewers.

Zine
For my zine, I wanted to create something reminiscent of a magazine layout because that is the type of graphic design I am most interested in. To implement this, I included a brief description styled in the form of a letter to the editor as well as only using my work that was layout and publication inspired.

Eco/Sustainability
For my prototype project I asked myself clothing, or threads of clothing. How can it be repurposed into more clothing or textile products, such as blankets and the like? Then I decided on creating yarn from these scraps that would then be repurposed back into the textiles industry.
Date 10 . 21
Speculative Design
With my speculative design project, entitled the Future of Tomorrow I decided on a community centered design. This project is an overall look into a world where global problems plaguing society are extinguished. It does so by speculating on the processes needed to go about doing so.
